Training & Development FAQs

Answers to common questions about how CICL designs and delivers learning programmes.

What types of Training & Development programmes do you offer?

We design and deliver programmes across leadership, HR, finance, operations, sales, customer service and data skills. Sessions can be open, in-house or fully customised for your organisation.

Can you customise training to our context and industry?

Yes. We begin with a needs analysis to understand your strategy, structure and realities, then design content, exercises and case studies that reflect your environment.

Do you deliver training virtually, onsite or hybrid?

We support all three. We’ll recommend the best mix based on your location, team size, budget and the level of interaction required.

How do you measure the impact of training?

We use pre- and post-programme assessments, feedback surveys, application tasks and manager check-ins to track learning and early behaviour changes, with reports to HR and leadership.

What is the typical duration of your programmes?

We run everything from focused 2–3 hour workshops to multi-week series and multi-month academies. Duration depends on the depth of skills to be built.

Can you help us build a long-term learning roadmap or academy?

Yes. We can help you design competency frameworks, learning paths and in-house academies that align with performance, growth and succession goals.

Data Protection

We handle participant information, feedback and evaluation data with the highest level of confidentiality and security. Only authorised CICL team members directly involved in your programmes can access this data.

All personally identifiable information is transmitted over encrypted channels and stored in secure systems using least-privilege access. We collect only what is necessary, keep it for agreed periods and avoid unnecessary sharing.

Any anonymised examples, case studies or statistics used for marketing or internal learning are carefully redacted to remove names and identifiable details.

Training recordings, feedback forms and evaluation reports are retained only for the duration agreed at the start of the engagement, after which they are securely deleted or archived in line with your policies.

Your trust is central to our work. Our processes are designed so you can confidently share the information we need to support your learning objectives.
