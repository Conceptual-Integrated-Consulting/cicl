FAQs

Clear answers to common questions about CICL Sales & Marketing programmes.

Can we run separate tracks for sales and marketing?

Yes. Many clients choose focused tracks for each team plus joint alignment sessions, so each group builds deep skills and still stays connected to the same strategy.

Is this suitable for both junior and experienced staff?

We calibrate content and exercises to the level of participants. Mixed cohorts also work well, as senior staff can model behaviours while juniors bring fresh questions and perspectives.

How long do Sales & Marketing programmes run?

Engagements range from single-focus workshops to multi-week academies with spaced sessions, coaching and live deal or campaign labs. We’ll recommend a format based on your goals.

Can sessions be delivered virtually?

Yes. We deliver fully virtual, fully in-person or blended formats. For geographically distributed teams, virtual sessions often work best, supplemented by in-person strategy or kick-off events.

Can you help us improve our CRM usage?

Absolutely. We incorporate sessions on CRM discipline, data hygiene and how to use tools effectively for coaching, forecasting and campaign optimisation.

What are the next steps if we are interested?

We start with a commercial discovery call to understand your current sales and marketing set-up, targets and constraints. From there, we design a tailored programme, including modules, timelines and success metrics.

Data Protection

In Sales & Marketing work, we may review sensitive commercial information such as customer lists, deal values and campaign performance. Any data shared with CICL is treated as confidential and used solely to support your engagement.

We limit access to authorised CICL consultants, store materials in secure environments and avoid including identifiable client details in training examples unless explicitly required and agreed.

Any reports or sample artefacts produced during our work can exclude names or financial details on request, while still preserving learning value for your teams.

Our goal is to support your growth while protecting your customers, partners and competitive information at every step.
