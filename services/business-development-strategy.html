Challenges

Strategy problems we see every day

We often meet organisations that are working very hard, but not always in the same direction. The patterns are familiar.

Great product, unclear positioning

You have something valuable, but your markets, segments and value proposition are not clearly defined or communicated.

Many ideas, weak business model

Multiple revenue ideas exist, but there is no coherent model for how value is created, delivered and captured sustainably.

Plans that never become initiatives

High-level documents exist, but they do not translate into specific projects, owners, timelines and metrics.

Branding that lags ambition

Your brand, messaging and visual identity no longer reflect where the organisation is or where it is going.

Wasteful, inconsistent processes

Operational processes consume time, money and goodwill, with frequent errors, delays and rework between teams.

Financial projections that feel like guesswork

Models exist mainly for presentations—not as tools to test assumptions, scenarios and capital decisions.

Weak reconciliation & audit practices

Boards and investors worry about the integrity of records, controls and reporting, especially during growth or change.

FAQs

Common questions about Business Development & Strategy

Do we need a fully defined vision before engaging CICL?

Not necessarily. We help you clarify vision and strategic direction as part of our work, especially where leadership has ideas but needs structure and alignment.

Can you support only one area, like financial modelling?

Yes. While many clients engage us across multiple areas, we also run focused projects—for example, market research, financial modelling or process optimisation only.

How do you work with internal strategy or finance teams?

We collaborate closely with internal teams, sharing frameworks, tools and analysis. Our role is to add capacity, structure and external perspective—not to replace them.

How long do strategy projects typically last?

Depending on scope, projects can range from 4–6 weeks for focused reviews to several months for full strategy, model and process redesigns, with agreed milestones.

Is your organisation working hard but not always in the same direction?

It may be a strategy and structure problem—not effort. Book a conversation with CICL to map where to focus first.
