FAQs

Common questions about Professional Training Programmes

Can programmes be customised to our organisation?

Yes. Most of our work is in-house and customised. We align content, cases and tools with your sector, roles and strategic priorities.

Do you offer certificates of completion?

We can issue certificates where appropriate, but our focus is on measurable behaviour change and business impact, not certificates alone.

What is the typical duration of a programme?

Programmes range from short workshops (half-day or 1–2 days) to multi-module academies over several weeks or months. We work with you to fit learning around operations.

Can individuals join if their organisation is not a client?

Yes. Our public / open programmes are designed for individuals and small teams who may not have an internal corporate programme.

Ready to align learning with your strategy?

If you are unsure where the biggest gaps are, start with a training needs and capability assessment. If you already know the pain points, we can design a focused programme around them.
