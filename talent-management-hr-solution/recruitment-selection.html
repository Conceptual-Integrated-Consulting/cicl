Challenges

Recruitment challenges we help you eliminate

We help you fix the hidden leaks in your hiring funnel—from unclear role definitions to weak assessments and poor candidate experience.

Vague role profiles

Roles defined by generic titles and long wishlists, rather than clear outcomes, competencies and success indicators.

Inconsistent screening

Different managers assessing candidates with different criteria, leading to biased or unpredictable hiring decisions.

Slow, messy processes

Candidates dropping off due to delayed feedback, unclear next steps or poor coordination between HR and hiring managers.

Poor quality of hire

New hires that look good on paper but struggle to perform or align with your culture once they join the organisation.

Recruitment & Selection FAQs

Clear answers to how CICL supports your hiring and selection process.

What types of roles do you recruit for?

We primarily focus on professional, specialist and management roles across corporate, development, operations and emerging tech functions. For high-volume or entry-level hiring, we can design the process and support your internal team to execute.

Can you manage the full recruitment process or just specific stages?

We can do both. Some clients retain CICL for end-to-end searches; others engage us for defined stages such as role scoping, shortlisting, interview design or final-stage selection support.

How do you charge for Recruitment & Selection?

Pricing depends on role complexity, volume and engagement model. We typically work with retainers, per-role search fees or project-based pricing, agreed after your initial discovery call.

How long does a typical recruitment engagement take?

Timelines vary by seniority and market conditions, but for most professional roles, you can expect shortlists within 2–4 weeks once the mandate is confirmed and the sourcing strategy is live.

How do you help reduce bias and improve fairness?

We use documented scorecards, structured interview guides and shared evaluation tools. We also encourage diverse interview panels and decisions based on evidence, not intuition alone.

Can you work alongside our existing HR and hiring managers?

Yes. Our typical model is collaborative—we act as an embedded partner to your HR and leadership teams, leaving you with stronger internal capability and reusable recruitment assets.

Data Protection

We handle candidate and organisational information with the highest level of confidentiality and security. Only authorised CICL team members directly involved in your mandate have access to your data.

All personally identifiable information is transmitted over encrypted channels and stored in secure systems using least-privilege access. We minimise what we collect, limit how long we retain it and avoid unnecessary sharing or exposure.

Any sample materials, anonymised reports or case studies used for training or marketing purposes are carefully redacted to remove names, companies and any identifiable details.

Candidate documents, interview notes and supporting files are retained only for the duration agreed at the start of the engagement, after which they are securely deleted from our active systems.

Your trust is central to our work. Our processes are designed so you can confidently share the information we need to support your hiring decisions.
