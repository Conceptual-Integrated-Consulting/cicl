Talent & Payroll Outsourcing FAQs

Clear answers to how our outsourcing model works in practice.

What does your Talent & Payroll Outsourcing service cover?

We provide staff leasing, HR administration, end-to-end payroll processing, statutory remittances, benefits administration, documentation and employee lifecycle support.

Do we retain control of daily work and performance?

Yes. You remain responsible for setting goals, assigning work and managing performance. We handle the employment, payroll and compliance backbone that supports those employees.

Can you manage payroll across multiple locations?

We support multi-location teams, operating within local regulations while maintaining standardised processes and reporting for leadership.

How are statutory remittances and taxes handled?

We calculate, deduct and remit statutory contributions and taxes in line with applicable regulations, keeping records and evidence for audits and reviews.

Can you support short-term projects or temporary headcount growth?

Yes. You can engage us for project-based staff leasing or interim outsourcing arrangements while you scale up or down.

How secure is our employee and payroll data?

Data is stored in secure systems, with restricted access, encryption and strict internal controls. We minimise what we collect, keep it only for agreed periods and follow clear retention and deletion policies.

Data Protection

We handle employee, payroll and compliance data with strict confidentiality and security. Only authorised CICL team members directly involved in your outsourcing engagement can access this information.

All personally identifiable data is transmitted over encrypted channels and stored in secure systems using least-privilege access. We collect only the data required to run your HR and payroll operations and keep it for agreed retention periods.

Any anonymised examples or metrics used for internal learning or marketing are carefully redacted to remove names and identifiable details.

Payroll records, reports and supporting documents are retained only for as long as is necessary to meet legal, regulatory and contractual obligations, after which they are securely deleted or archived.

Your trust and your employees’ trust are central to our work. Our processes are designed so that you can confidently delegate HR and payroll operations to us.
